Ursula Pokorny is a curator from Vienna, Austria.
Currently, she is writing her dissertation on artist Ceija Stojka and curating the art collection of Oesterreichische Nationalbank. Previously, Ursula has worked for Kunstsammlung Nordrhein-Westfalen in Düsseldorf, Fondazione Sandretto Re Rebaudengo in Turin, the Museum of Modern Art in New York, Kunsthalle Basel, Galerie Martin Janda, and the Academy of Fine Arts in Vienna.
Ursula holds an M.A. from the Center for Curatorial Studies at Bard College, New York and a B.A. from the University of Applied Arts, Vienna. mail@ursulapokorny.com
