Ursula Pokorny is a curator from Vienna, Austria. Her research focuses on architectural heritage and artists’ estates. Currently, she is Assistant Curator at Kunstsammlung Nordrhein-Westfalen in Düsseldorf. Previously, Ursula has worked for the Museum of Modern Art in New York, Kunsthalle Basel, Galerie Martin Janda, and the Academy of Fine Arts in Vienna. Ursula holds an M.A. from the Center for Curatorial Studies at Bard College, New York and a B.A. from the University of Applied Arts, Vienna.
